ABOUT US

LEARNSTYLE+.COM is a specialized web-based indicator designed to identify and categorize individual learning styles. Our mission is to help students understand how they process information best—whether visually, through sound, through social interaction, or through independent study.

This project is a product for our school's contest, developed by students of Emilio Aguinaldo College-Manila, 12 STEM 6, Group Technology 1.
